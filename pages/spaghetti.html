Recipes | Classic spaghetti aglio e olio

    Ingredients:
  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
    How to prepare:
  1. Cook the spaghetti in a pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until the garlic turns golden (be careful not to burn it).
  3. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the garlic-infused oil, season with salt and pepper, and serve.
